Library Expectations

  • Quiet voices at all times
  • No eating or drinking
  • Clean up after yourself
  • Listen to Ms. Downs, Ms. Kiino, or any other adult in the room
  • If you pull a book from a shelf and do not want it, put it on top of the bookcase to be reshelved by Ms. Kiino

Overdue and Lost Books

If you have an overdue or lost book, you may not check out until the book is returned, you pay to replace it, or you make other arrangements with Ms. Downs. Look for the book in your classroom and at home. If you still can't find it, speak with Ms. Downs.
